FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

+

Our standard MOQ is 100 pieces per style for most products. However, we can accommodate smaller quantities for premium custom projects or sample orders. The MOQ may vary depending on the complexity of the design, materials used, and production requirements.

What is the typical lead time for production?

+

Lead times vary based on order complexity and quantity:

  • Sample Development: 7-15 days
  • Small Orders (100-500 pcs): 15-25 days
  • Medium Orders (500-2000 pcs): 25-35 days
  • Large Orders (2000+ pcs): 35-45 days

Rush orders can be accommodated with additional fees.

Do you provide samples before production?

+

Yes, we provide samples for approval before mass production. We offer:

  • Existing Samples: Free (shipping costs apply)
  • Custom Samples: $50-200 per sample (refundable with order)
  • Pre-production Samples: Included in order cost

All samples must be approved before production begins.

What quality certifications do you have?

+

We maintain multiple international certifications:

  • ISO 9001:2015 - Quality Management System
  • ISO 14001 - Environmental Management
  • BSCI - Social Compliance
  • LWG Gold Medal - Leather Working Group
  • OEKO-TEX Standard 100 - Textile Safety
  • REACH Compliance - Chemical Safety

Can you handle custom packaging and labeling?

+

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive packaging and labeling services:

  • Custom Labels: Woven, printed, embossed, or metal labels
  • Packaging Design: Custom boxes, dust bags, and protective materials
  • Branding Elements: Logo placement, brand tags, and authenticity cards
  • Retail Ready: Barcode labels, price tags, and retail packaging

What payment terms do you offer?

+

We offer flexible payment terms based on order value and client relationship:

  • New Clients: 50% deposit, 50% before shipment
  • Established Clients: 30% deposit, 70% before shipment
  • Large Orders: Customized payment schedules available
  • Payment Methods: T/T, L/C, PayPal, Western Union
