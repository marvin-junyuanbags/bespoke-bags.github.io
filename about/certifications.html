TESTING CAPABILITIES & LABORATORY ACCREDITATION

State-of-the-Art Testing Facilities

Our in-house testing laboratory is equipped with advanced equipment and staffed by certified technicians to ensure comprehensive quality control and compliance testing.

Accredited Testing Capabilities:

  • Physical Testing: Tensile strength, tear resistance, abrasion testing
  • Chemical Analysis: Heavy metals, formaldehyde, azo dyes, pH levels
  • Colorfastness Testing: Light, washing, rubbing, perspiration
  • Dimensional Stability: Shrinkage, stretch, recovery testing
  • Hardware Testing: Corrosion resistance, plating adhesion, functionality
  • Packaging Testing: Drop tests, compression, vibration testing

Laboratory Accreditations:

  • ISO/IEC 17025:2017 - General requirements for testing laboratories
  • CNAS (China National Accreditation Service) - Laboratory accreditation
  • ILAC (International Laboratory Accreditation Cooperation) - Mutual recognition
