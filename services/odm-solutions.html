ODM VS OEM: UNDERSTANDING THE DIFFERENCE

ODM (Original Design Manufacturing)

We design and manufacture

  • Complete product development
  • Market research and analysis
  • Original design creation
  • Material selection and sourcing
  • Manufacturing and quality control
  • Packaging and branding support
  • Market-ready products

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturing)

You design, we manufacture

  • Manufacturing to your specifications
  • Production of your designs
  • Quality control and testing
  • Packaging according to requirements
  • Delivery to your specifications
  • Limited design input
  • Focus on production efficiency
