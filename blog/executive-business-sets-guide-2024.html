Collection Care and Maintenance

Proper maintenance of executive business sets ensures longevity, appearance retention, and continued professional presentation value:

Comprehensive Care Protocols

Daily Maintenance Routine
End-of-Day Care:
  • Content Removal: Empty all compartments and remove temporary items
  • Surface Cleaning: Gentle wipe-down with appropriate leather cleaner
  • Shape Maintenance: Proper storage positioning to maintain structural integrity
  • Hardware Check: Inspect zippers, clasps, and moving parts for smooth operation
  • Moisture Control: Ensure complete drying if exposed to moisture during use
Storage Preparation:
  • Use dust bags or protective covers for each piece
  • Maintain proper spacing to prevent pressure marks
  • Insert shape-maintaining forms in structured pieces
  • Ensure adequate ventilation in storage areas
Weekly Deep Care
Thorough Cleaning Process:
  • Complete Inspection: Detailed examination for wear, damage, or maintenance needs
  • Deep Cleaning: Comprehensive cleaning using appropriate leather care products
  • Conditioning Treatment: Application of leather conditioner to maintain suppleness
  • Hardware Maintenance: Cleaning and lubrication of metal components
  • Interior Care: Cleaning and freshening of interior compartments and linings
Preventive Measures:
  • Rotation of pieces to ensure even wear patterns
  • Application of protective treatments as needed
  • Documentation of any maintenance requirements or concerns
  • Professional service scheduling for complex maintenance needs
